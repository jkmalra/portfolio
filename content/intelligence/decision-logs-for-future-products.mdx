A product with memory is easier to trust than one that only exposes the latest state. Decision logs preserve reasoning over time and make future systems easier to audit and extend.

## Key takeaways

- Decision memory reduces repeated confusion.
- Accountability improves when context is preserved.
- Logs are a design and systems artifact, not just operations residue.
