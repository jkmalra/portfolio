Trust slows teams down only when it is postponed until after product direction is already fixed. The better model is to design governance into the operating layer from the beginning.

## Context

Many teams still think trust work begins after shipping pressure appears. In reality, the earlier governance enters the product conversation, the cheaper and more effective it becomes.

## Key takeaways

- Governance is faster when it is designed upstream.
- Case studies can prove trust strategy through execution detail.
- Velocity and responsibility are not opposing modes.
