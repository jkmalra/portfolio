Navigation becomes strategy when each route is designed for a different evaluation moment. When everything collapses into a single feed, a portfolio stops feeling intentional.

## Key takeaways

- Route clarity shapes first impressions.
- Audience-specific contexts reduce friction.
- Separation creates premium calm.
